Artist of the Month

Every Month we celebrate the work of one local artists. This month we have chosen: Triz Magosara!

Trix Magosara

Hi, my name is Trixia Magosara a Filipino Ceramic Designer-Maker in the United Kingdom. I am a graduate of Central Saint Martins - University of the Arts London in BA (hons) Ceramic Design with First Class Honours in 2021. I am currently based in Thornwood, Epping, Essex. I make my ceramic pieces in my studio surrounded by beautiful nature and 3 lakes at Thornwood Springs.
